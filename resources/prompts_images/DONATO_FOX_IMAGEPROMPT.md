# Patrulheiro Donato Fox — image prompt (nano banana)

An extreme chibi super-deformed (SD) figurine of a young industrial-security patrolman, styled like a small collectible vinyl figure (Funko Pop / nendoroid). VERY IMPORTANT PROPORTIONS: the head is enormous, round and as large as the entire rest of the body; the whole character is only about three heads tall; tiny torso, very short stubby arms and legs. This is NOT a realistic human body.

He is a twenty-eight-year-old beat-patrol guard for an industrial recycling cartel, weary and ambivalent, just wanting his shift to end. His huge round head has a bored, slightly slumped young-man face with a faint five-o-clock shadow, droopy tired eyes, and short dark hair under a tilted-back peaked cap. He wears a worn corporate-security uniform in dingy olive-green and grey: a utility jacket with a faded cartel patch, a light armored vest, a duty belt with a radio and baton, and scuffed boots. Grime and old coffee stains. His stubby hands are tucked lazily into his pockets, one holding a paper cup.

Art style: cel-shaded anime 3D, low-poly stylized, somber cyber-gothic mood. Full body head to feet, centered, facing camera, plain pure-white (#FFFFFF) background, flat even lighting. REMINDER — extreme chibi: gigantic round head equal to whole body, three heads tall, tiny stubby limbs. Do NOT draw realistic adult proportions.
