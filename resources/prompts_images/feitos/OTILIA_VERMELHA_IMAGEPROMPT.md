An extreme chibi super-deformed (SD) figurine of a displaced refugee mother searching for her lost daughter, styled like a small collectible vinyl figure (Funko Pop / nendoroid). VERY IMPORTANT PROPORTIONS: the head is enormous, round and as large as the entire rest of the body; only about three heads tall; tiny torso, very short stubby limbs. NOT a realistic human body.

Mood: displaced, grieving-but-determined, somber, dusty-red accent.
Art style: cel-shaded anime 3D, low-poly stylized, somber cyber-gothic. Full body, centered, facing camera, plain pure-white (#FFFFFF) background, flat even lighting.
