# Vanda do Café — image prompt (nano banana)

An extreme chibi super-deformed (SD) figurine of a 47-year-old woman, styled like a small collectible vinyl figure (Funko Pop / nendoroid). VERY IMPORTANT PROPORTIONS: the head is enormous, round and as large as the entire rest of the body; only about three heads tall; tiny torso, very short stubby limbs. NOT a realistic adult body. She is a chatty itinerant street-coffee vendor in the city core, a cheerful hub of neighborhood gossip who knows everyone's routine. Huge round head with a lively, warm, talkative face, bright knowing eyes, a wide friendly grin, rosy cheeks, and curly dark hair tied up in a colorful kerchief. She wears practical vendor's work clothes in warm worn tones — an apron over a buttoned blouse and skirt, sleeves rolled up, fingerless mitts, and comfortable shoes, with a small portable coffee-urn rig strapped to her tiny back trailing a wisp of steam. Tin cups and a coin-pouch dangle from her apron. Stubby hands hold out a steaming cup of coffee.

Art style: cel-shaded anime 3D, low-poly stylized, somber cyber-gothic, warm muted street-vendor palette. Full body, centered, facing camera, plain pure-white (#FFFFFF) background, flat even lighting. REMINDER — extreme chibi: gigantic round head equal to the whole body, three heads tall, tiny stubby limbs. Do NOT draw realistic adult proportions.
