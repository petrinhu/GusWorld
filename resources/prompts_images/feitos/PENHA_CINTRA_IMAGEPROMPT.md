# Penha Cintra — image prompt (nano banana)

An extreme chibi super-deformed (SD) figurine of a 45-year-old woman, styled like a small collectible vinyl figure (Funko Pop / nendoroid). VERY IMPORTANT PROPORTIONS: the head is enormous, round and as large as the entire rest of the body; only about three heads tall; tiny torso, very short stubby limbs. NOT a realistic adult body. She is a clandestine analog-radio relay operator from a remote underground cell of silence-keepers. Huge round head with a steady, focused, careworn face, attentive eyes, and dark hair streaked with grey pulled into a practical low bun. She wears layered earth-toned work clothes — a worn olive utility jacket over a grey shirt — with chunky vintage headphones around her neck and a coil of magnetic tape tucked in a pocket. A long knotted rope cord of many small knots loops at her waist. Stubby hands hold a small battered analog radio with a glowing dial and a stub antenna.

Art style: cel-shaded anime 3D, low-poly stylized, somber cyber-gothic, muted analog-tech palette. Full body, centered, facing camera, plain pure-white (#FFFFFF) background, flat even lighting. REMINDER — extreme chibi: gigantic round head equal to the whole body, three heads tall, tiny stubby limbs. Do NOT draw realistic adult proportions.
