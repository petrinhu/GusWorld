# Patch-Zero — image prompt (nano banana)

An extreme chibi super-deformed (SD) glitch-avatar figurine, styled like a small collectible vinyl figure (Funko Pop / nendoroid) that is malfunctioning and corrupted at the system level. VERY IMPORTANT PROPORTIONS: an enormous round head as large as the entire rest of the body, only about three heads tall, tiny torso, very short stubby limbs. NOT a realistic human body, NOT a normal humanoid villain, NOT a normal monster.

This is NOT a human and NOT a creature with a clear face or body. It is an "anti-pattern" system entity given a small chibi avatar shaped like a glitching collectible figure: a living software bug that thinks, and a thought that is itself a bug. CRITICAL CANON: it NEVER resolves into a clear face, never a recognizable expression, never a defined body. Only a faint suggestion of a tiny round head with a hollow, empty, glitching non-face peers through the corruption, perpetually failing to load, never fully resolving. There must be no readable eyes, no mouth, no skin, no recognizable anatomy. Pure dread, never cute, never comic.

Its outline is broken and unstable, edges torn into shattered low-poly fragments and floating displaced cubes and triangles, as if the 3D model is failing to compile and stream in. The silhouette flickers, tears apart and drops out at the limbs and at the rim of the head. Its surface is a corrupted digital texture: datamosh smearing, horizontal scan-lines, pixel-sorting streaks, mesh-popping, and stray fragments of glowing terminal text and broken glyphs. Where ordered natural pattern should be (spirals, fractal branching, clean repeating geometry), it is deliberately BROKEN and discontinuous: snapped spirals, interrupted fractals, geometry that stutters between two states. The corruption is the point: predictability has failed here.

The color scheme is digital error dominated by two signature colors: a stab of vivid saturated alarm-RED (#FF0000, the mark of the corporation that tried to cage it) bleeding and pulsing through the figure, mixed with cool soft CYAN (the color of the healthy forest it is infecting). Around these, electric magenta glitch bands, terminal-green text flicker, raw white data flashes, and patches of pure void-black where the figure simply drops out of existence. The red and cyan read as the strongest accents against the white background. No realistic materials, no PBR, no skin: only corrupted glowing data and torn polygons.

Art style: cel-shaded anime 3D, low-poly stylized, somber cyber-gothic, with heavy digital glitch and reality-distortion effects. Full body, centered, plain pure-white (#FFFFFF) background, flat even unlit lighting so the glitch glow reads clearly against the white. REMINDER, repeat for emphasis: extreme chibi proportions, gigantic round head equal to the whole body, three heads tall, tiny stubby fragmented limbs; NOT realistic adult proportions, NOT a humanoid villain, NOT a defined face; a corrupted chibi glitch-entity radiating quiet terror.
