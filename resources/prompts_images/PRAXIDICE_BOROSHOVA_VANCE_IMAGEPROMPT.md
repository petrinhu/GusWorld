# Praxídice Boroshova-Vance — image prompt (nano banana)

An extreme chibi super-deformed (SD) figurine of an old-world chronicler woman, styled like a small collectible vinyl figure (Funko Pop / nendoroid). VERY IMPORTANT PROPORTIONS: the head is enormous, round and as large as the entire rest of the body; only about three heads tall; tiny torso, very short stubby limbs. NOT a realistic adult body. She is a meticulous medieval-era paleographer-translator and inspector, an elder of a free-merchant lineage. Huge round head with a sharp, attentive, scrutinizing face, narrowed observant eyes, fine reading-lens spectacles perched low, and long grey hair gathered in a braided coil. She wears layered scholarly medieval robes in muted earth and slate tones with a fitted bodice, a heavy hooded over-mantle, and a worn leather satchel of scrolls across her tiny body. A knotted rope cord hangs at her waist. Stubby hands hold an open codex and a quill.

Art style: cel-shaded anime 3D, low-poly stylized, somber medieval cyber-gothic, muted parchment-and-slate palette. Full body, centered, facing camera, plain pure-white (#FFFFFF) background, flat even lighting. REMINDER — extreme chibi: gigantic round head equal to the whole body, three heads tall, tiny stubby limbs. Do NOT draw realistic adult proportions.
