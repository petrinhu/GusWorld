# Obsidian Wall Texture — image prompt (nano banana)

A seamless, tileable material texture swatch (no object, no character, no background — just the repeating surface filling the whole frame). The material is a wall of dark polished obsidian / volcanic glass: a deep near-black surface with subtle dark-grey veining and sharp angular fractured facets, with faint oily iridescent reflections. Running through it are thin glowing cyan runic engravings that pulse with soft light.

Art style: stylized, cel-shaded-friendly flat material (low-poly game texture, not photoreal). Shown perfectly flat and front-on as a square that tiles seamlessly on all edges, even flat lighting across the whole surface so it can repeat without visible seams.
